Field experiences

    (CTD, ADCP, DO sampling)

    • R/V ONNURI (Jul 2022)

  • The Korea/Tsushima Strait
    (Induced voltage measurement on submarine cable)

    • Voltage measurement was conducted on the submarine cable between Busan, Republic of Korea, and Hamada, Japan, to estimate the volume transport through the Korea/Tsushima Strait. This was achieved by measuring the motion-induced voltage on the submarine cable as it crossed the strait. However, on May 17, 2017, the voltage observations were halted due to a failure in the measurement equipment. The observations were resumed on Dec 13, 2020, thanks to the efforts of Jihwan Kim, who devised and built a new observation solution, developed a new data analysis method, and set up a cloud data server.
